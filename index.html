Welcome to this study

We are interested in learning about how people form social connections online. In this study you will meet and interact with other participants online by answering questions about yourself and things you like to do.

Welcome to this study

The other participants in this study are other people who are also connected to this site via the internet.

You will not have any information about the other people you'll be interacting with, other than what they choose to share with you. Similarly, other participants will not have any information about you other than what you choose to share with them.
